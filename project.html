AV Studio

AV Studio is my freelance photography company. We have been operating in the photography industry since 2018 and are currently based in Penang. Our services cover both Penang and Kedah. AV Studio specializes in three main types of photography services: convocations, events, and weddings. In 2023, my 5 groupmates and I completed a digital marketing and content management project aimed at developing the main website, managing all content, and enhancing social media marketing for the company, AV Studio. This project commenced with the assistance of my lecturer, Sir Hilmi Abu Bakar, and was completed within 3 months. In this project, we used figma and framer software to publish the website.

The creation of a dedicated website has significantly boosted our online visibility, making it easier for potential clients to find and engage with our services. The website also serves as a comprehensive portfolio, displaying our best work in convocations, events, and weddings photography, which helps attract new clients. Our social media presence has grown, with more followers and higher engagement rates on Instagram, and TikTok platform. The improved online presence and marketing efforts have attracted new clients, leading to a steady increase in bookings for our services.

The project's success has set a foundation for long-term growth, ensuring that AV Studio remains competitive in the photography industry. From developing a robust online presence to enhancing social media marketing efforts, the outcomes have been overwhelmingly positive. This project has not only driven business growth but also enhanced our skills in web development and social media marketing.

Gold Hunter

Game

During my studies for a diploma in New Media Communication and Content Entrepreneurship, my group and I were assigned a project in the game development subject to create a 2D pixelated game using Unreal Engine 5. My group, Dimensional Studio, decided to develop the "Gold Hunter" game. The concept for "Gold Hunter" was inspired by the classic mobile game Temple Run. We aimed to infuse it with a nostalgic pixel art style while incorporating a top-down perspective to enhance the gameplay experience. With the guidance of our game development lecturer, it took us a year to complete the game. My role within the team was to create blueprints for all game functions. This involved designing the core mechanics, collision detection, and item collection. Additionally, I was tasked with troubleshooting and resolving any errors or issues that arose during gameplay testing.

Every year, my course will hold a game exhibition event called "GameINC". The purpose of this event is to showcase all self-made games from semester 4 students. This event was held at UiTM Lendu, Alor Gajah, Melaka from 11 until 13 July 2024. The event attracted numerous visitors, both from inside and outside of UiTM, who served as beta testers for the 10 games featured. Among the games showcased, our game, "Gold Hunter" stood out and earned the Best Game award in 2024.

Throughout this assignment, developing "Gold Hunter" significantly enhanced my proficiency with Unreal Engine 5, particularly in creating and managing game blueprints. Addressing and resolving gameplay issues honed my problem-solving skills, allowing me to think critically and creatively under pressure. Working within a team taught me valuable project management skills, including time management, task delegation, and effective communication.

The development of "Gold Hunter" was a transformative experience in my journey as a game designer. The project not only allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge in a practical setting but also equipped me with essential skills for my future career. The recognition and success at the GameINC exhibition underscored the impact of our efforts and inspired me to continue pursuing excellence in game development.

MultiMaker

Resources

Multimaker Resources is an innovative company that specializes in providing educational solutions and cutting-edge technology in the realms of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) and the Fine Arts. The company aims to empower learners of all ages by integrating traditional education with modern technological advancements, such as 3D printing and laser cutting, to foster a more engaging and comprehensive learning experience.

I hold the position of lead photographer and creative editor at this company. I joined the team in 2023, and my most recent project involved managing the W.H.O lab kits photoshoot. This project was commissioned by our client, Universiti Sains Malaya (USM). This project involved capturing over 20 lab kits to be featured on their website for selling purposes.

In this project, I used my Canon R, a mirrorless camera with high resolution, ensures sharp images. For the lens I used 50mm prime lenses with an aperture of f4 for capturing fine details of the lab kit. As for the software, I use Adobe Lightroom or Photoshop to enhance sharpness get best quality out of those images. By focusing on these equipment, software, I ensure that the photos not only look professional but also enhance the user experience and contribute to the overall success for their website.
